Delicious Spaghetti Recipe

This is a simple and delicious spaghetti recipe that you can make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Add canned tomatoes and dried oregano.
  5. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until sauce thickens.
  6. Drain the spaghetti and toss with the sauce.
  7. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.

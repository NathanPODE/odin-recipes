Delicious Cheeseburger Recipe

This is a simple and delicious cheeseburger recipe that you can make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Ketchup and mustard to taste

Steps:

  1. Form the ground beef into 4 patties.
  2. Season the patties with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or pan-fry the patties for 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty and let it melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns and place them on the bottom of each burger.
  6. Add lettuce, tomato, and onion slices to each burger.
  7. Top with the cheeseburger patties and serve with ketchup and mustard if desired.

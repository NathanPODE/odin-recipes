Delicious Pizza Recipe

This is a simple and delicious pizza recipe that you can make at home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, and knead the dough until smooth.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  5. Roll out the dough and add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
